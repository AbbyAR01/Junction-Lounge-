Our Story

More Than Just a Restaurant

Located right at Ekon Junction in Cape Coast, Junction Lounge is where great food meets great company. Whether you're stopping in for a family outing, a romantic date night, or an office team bonding event — we have everything you need for an unforgettable time.

Our kitchen serves an incredible spread of local Ghanaian favourites, fresh seafood, international dishes, cocktails and more — all prepared with love and care.
